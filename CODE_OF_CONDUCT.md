# Code of Conduct

We're here to help people fix broken computers. Let's keep it productive.

## Our Rules

- **Be helpful.** Everyone here is trying to solve a problem.
- **Be patient.** Not everyone is a Linux expert. That's the point of this tool.
- **Be constructive.** Criticism is welcome, toxicity is not.
- **No spam.** Issues and PRs should be about RedSeek Rescue.

## Enforcement

If someone's being a problem, reach out to **rednic@ltos.ro**. We'll handle it.

---

**Short version:** Don't be a jerk. Help people fix their Windows.
